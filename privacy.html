Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-12 · Sakina app · Operated by Ahmed Basolan ("Service Provider")

Your spiritual journey belongs to you. Everything you log in Sakina — your moods, reflections, and history — is stored privately for your benefit only. We do not analyse it, sell it, share it, or use it for advertising. It exists solely to serve you within the app. Your written reflections and journal entries stay on your device.

Information We Collect and Why

We collect only what is necessary for the app to function:

  • Email address — to create and secure your account.
  • Mood entries and spiritual history — stored privately in your account to power your streak, history view, and personalised guidance. We never read, analyse, or share this data.
  • Reflections and journal entries — your private writing is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Location — to calculate accurate prayer times and qibla direction for your area, Sakina can use your device's location. Depending on the permission you grant, this may be approximate (your city) or precise (GPS) location. Your coordinates are used to determine your city and compute prayer times; we do not store your GPS coordinates on our servers. You can also decline location access and enter your city manually instead.
  • Notification preference — whether you want reminders and at what time, used only to send the reminders you opted into.
  • Subscription status — whether your account is free or premium. We do not collect payment card details or billing information (see Payments below).
  • Crash and error diagnostics (optional, off by default) — if you turn on crash reporting in Settings, when the app hits an error we send technical details (device type, OS version, error message and stack trace) to help us find and fix bugs. These reports may include your account identifier and city for debugging. We do not track which screens you visit or how long you use the app, and your mood or journal content is never included. This is disabled until you opt in, and you can turn it off at any time.
  • IP address — may be captured by our infrastructure providers as part of standard network communication.

Your Payments — We Don't See Them

Subscriptions are processed entirely by the app store you purchased through — Google Play (Android) or the Apple App Store (iOS). The store handles your payment card and billing details — the Service Provider never receives or stores that information. RevenueCat is used only to confirm whether your subscription is active. RevenueCat receives a pseudonymous subscriber identifier (your in-app account ID) and a purchase-receipt confirmation; it does not receive your name, card number, or store account email.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Application or its third-party SDKs may use device identifiers and similar technologies to support diagnostics and service delivery. Where required by applicable law, the Service Provider will obtain consent before using non-essential tracking technologies.

Third-Party Services

The Service Provider shares data with the following services only as necessary to operate the app. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — secure cloud storage for your account, mood history, and progress.
  • Google Play Billing / Apple App Store — processes your subscription payment.
  • RevenueCat — subscription status confirmation only.
  • PostHog — crash and error diagnostics (only if you opt in).
  • Expo — push notification delivery.
  • Aladhan API — receives your city and country to return prayer times.

The Service Provider does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other party for commercial purposes. Information may be disclosed only as required by law, to protect rights or safety, or to prevent fraud.

Data Retention

  • Account, mood, and progress data: retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, this data is permanently removed from our servers without undue delay.
  • Crash and error diagnostics: retained for up to 24 months, then deleted.
  • Aggregated anonymised data: may be retained indefinitely as it no longer identifies you.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored on servers outside your country of residence. Where applicable law requires safeguards for international transfers (including transfers outside the EEA), the Service Provider relies on Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission or other legally recognised mechanisms.

Security

The Service Provider uses encryption in transit (HTTPS) and encryption at rest on our servers, and applies physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your information. Data stored only on your device (such as your reflections) is protected by your device's own security. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, the Service Provider will notify you in accordance with applicable legal requirements.

Children

The Application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact the Service Provider at ahmed.basolan97@gmail.com and it will be deleted immediately.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting the Service Provider. You can also delete your account from within the app (Settings → Delete Account), which permanently removes your personal data and spiritual history from our servers. You may also request deletion by emailing ahmed.basolan97@gmail.com.

To stop further data collection, you can uninstall the Application. Uninstalling stops future collection but does not automatically delete data already stored on our servers — use the in-app Delete Account option or contact us to request that.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell your data), and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. To exercise your CCPA/CPRA rights, contact the Service Provider at ahmed.basolan97@gmail.com.

Your Consent

Where processing is based on your consent (for example, push notifications or crash reporting), you provide that consent by opting in. You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting processing carried out before withdrawal.

Changes to This Policy

The Service Provider may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated inside the app before they take effect. The effective date above will be updated with each revision. Previous versions are available upon request.
