Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-16 · Sakina app · Operated by Ahmed Basolan ("Service Provider")

Your spiritual journey belongs to you. Everything you log in Sakina — your moods, reflections, and history — is stored privately for your benefit only. We do not analyse it, sell it, share it, or use it for advertising. It exists solely to serve you within the app.

Information We Collect and Why

We collect only what is necessary for the app to function:

  • Email address — to create and secure your account.
  • Mood entries and spiritual history — stored privately in your account to power your streak, history view, and personalised guidance. We never read, analyse, or share this data.
  • City and country (not GPS) — you type this in manually so we can calculate your local prayer times. The app does not access your device's location services or precise GPS coordinates.
  • Notification preference — whether you want reminders and at what time, used only to send the reminders you opted into.
  • Subscription status — whether your account is free or premium. We do not collect payment card details or billing information (see Payments below).
  • Anonymous usage data — device type, OS version, screens visited, and time spent in the app, collected without personal identifiers. Used only to find and fix issues. Your mood or journal content is never included in analytics.
  • IP address — may be captured by our infrastructure providers as part of standard network communication.

Your Payments — We Don't See Them

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store). Apple handles your payment card and billing details — the Service Provider never receives or stores that information. RevenueCat is used only to confirm whether your subscription is active. RevenueCat receives an anonymous subscriber ID and a purchase receipt confirmation; it does not receive your name, card number, or Apple ID email.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Application or its third-party SDKs may use device identifiers and similar technologies to support analytics and service delivery. Where required by applicable law, the Service Provider will obtain consent before using non-essential tracking technologies.

Third-Party Services

The Service Provider shares data with the following services only as necessary to operate the app. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — secure cloud storage for your account and private data.
  • RevenueCat — subscription status confirmation only.
  • PostHog — anonymous usage analytics.
  • Expo — push notification delivery.
  • Aladhan API — receives your city and country to return prayer times.

The Service Provider does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other party for commercial purposes. Information may be disclosed only as required by law, to protect rights or safety, or to prevent fraud.

Data Retention

  • Account and mood data: retained for as long as your account is active, plus 12 months after you delete it, unless longer retention is required by law.
  • Anonymous analytics data: retained for up to 24 months.
  • Aggregated anonymised data: may be retained indefinitely as it no longer identifies you.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored on servers outside your country of residence. Where applicable law requires safeguards for international transfers (including transfers outside the EEA), the Service Provider relies on Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission or other legally recognised mechanisms.

Security

The Service Provider uses encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest, and applies physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your information. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, the Service Provider will notify you in accordance with applicable legal requirements.

Children

The Application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact the Service Provider at ahmed.basolan97@gmail.com and it will be deleted immediately.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting the Service Provider. You can also delete your account from within the app, which removes your personal data and spiritual history from our servers.

To stop further data collection, you can uninstall the Application. Uninstalling stops future collection but does not automatically delete data already stored on our servers — contact us to request that.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell your data), and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. To exercise your CCPA/CPRA rights, contact the Service Provider at ahmed.basolan97@gmail.com.

Your Consent

Where processing is based on your consent (for example, push notifications), you provide that consent by opting in. You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting processing carried out before withdrawal.

Changes to This Policy

The Service Provider may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated inside the app before they take effect. The effective date above will be updated with each revision. Previous versions are available upon request.
